Introduction

When it comes to buying crypto, there are a lot of factors that come into play, such as location and protocol. Some cryptocurrencies are harder to obtain than others. Vara Network is one of them. Vara Network is not currently supported across Coinbase’s vast ecosystem. There are still ways you can find a way to buy Vara Network. Sign up with Coinbase to stay up to date on Vara Network and get notified when it becomes available on Coinbase app.

Step-by-step

  1. 1

    Check CoinMarketCap to see where you can buy Vara Network and with which currencies

    For each cryptocurrency, CoinMarketCap provides a list of purchasing options (also known as market pairs). Go to CoinMarketCap and search for Vara Network. Tap on the button labeled “Market” near the price chart. In this view, you will see a complete list of places you can purchase Vara Network as well as the currencies you can use to obtain it. Under “Pairs” you'll see the shorthand for Vara Network, VARA, plus a second currency. The second currency is what you can use to purchase Vara Network. If you'd like to purchase VARA with the U.S. Dollar, look for VARA/USD.

  1. 2

    Pick a platform to make your purchase

    Different platforms have different levels of security, reliability, and liquidity. Before creating an account, do your research.

  1. 3

    Make the purchase on your chosen platform

    Every platform has a different way of doing things. Some platforms are very easy to use, others not so much.

    Generally speaking, purchasing crypto with a fiat currency like the U.S. Dollar will be easier than purchasing it with another crypto.

    If you do have to purchase Vara Network with another crypto, you’ll need to first create a crypto wallet that supports Vara Network, then you’ll buy the first currency and use it to buy Vara Network on the platform you chose.

    If you get stuck, most platforms provide guides. But if they don’t, there is a vibrant community of crypto enthusiasts that have likely posted guides on Youtube, Twitter, and elsewhere.
